Quick Start Guide

Welcome to GateFlux! This guide will help you get started with the app based on who you are.

Who are you?

Select your role to see the right getting started steps:

I'm a Resident

Living in the society and want to manage visitors, view notices, and access community features.

I'm a Security Guard

Working at the society gate and need to check visitors in and out.

I'm an Admin

Managing the society, residents, security staff, and settings.

For Residents

Step 1: Download the App or Access Web Portal

1
Get the GateFlux app
Download GateFlux from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android). Search for "GateFlux" or ask your society admin for a direct link.

Alternatively, access the web portal at your society's GateFlux URL.

Step 2: Create Account or Log In

Self-Registration Available

If your society has enabled self-registration, you can create your own account by clicking "Sign up" on the login page and selecting your society from the dropdown.

1
Open the app and enter your phone number
Use the phone number registered with your society.
2
Enter the OTP
You'll receive a one-time password via SMS. Enter it to verify your number.
3
Select your flat
If you own or rent multiple flats, choose the one you want to manage.
You're in!

Once logged in, you'll see your dashboard with visitor activity, notices, and quick actions.

Web Portal Access

Prefer using a browser? You can also log in via the web portal using email/password or Mobile OTP. See Web Portal Login for details.

For Security Guards

Step 1: Get Your Login

1
Your admin will create your account
The society admin will add you to the system and give you login credentials.
2
Download the GateFlux Guard app
Get it from the App Store or Google Play Store (search "GateFlux Guard").
3
Log in with your credentials
Enter your username/phone and password provided by the admin.

Step 2: Know Your Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:

  • Expected Visitors - Pre-approved visitors for today
  • Currently Inside - Visitors who haven't checked out yet
  • Scan QR - Quick button to scan visitor QR codes
  • Walk-in Entry - Register unexpected visitors

Step 3: Try Your First Check-in

1
When a visitor arrives, tap "Scan QR"
Point your camera at the visitor's QR code (from their pre-approval).
2
Verify the details
The screen shows the visitor name, flat number, and resident name. Confirm it matches.
3
Tap "Check In"
The visitor is now logged as inside the society.
No QR code?

If the visitor doesn't have a QR code, use the search function or register them as a walk-in. See the Security Guard Guide for all scenarios.

For Admins

Step 1: Access the Admin Panel

1
Open the GateFlux web dashboard
Go to your society's GateFlux URL (e.g., yourSociety.gateflux.co) in a web browser.
2
Log in with your admin credentials
Your super admin or GateFlux support will have provided these.

Step 2: Essential Setup Tasks

As a new admin, here's what you should do first:

  1. Add residents - Import or manually add your society's residents
  2. Add security staff - Create accounts for your guards
  3. Set up service providers - Add common providers (maids, cooks, drivers)
  4. Post a welcome notice - Let residents know GateFlux is live
Important

Make sure residents have the correct phone numbers in the system - that's how they log in!

Enable Self-Registration

Allow residents to register themselves instead of manually adding each one:

  1. Request from Platform Admin - Ask your GateFlux platform admin to enable "Allow Self-Signup" for your society
  2. Notify residents - Once enabled, residents can click "Sign up" on your login page
  3. Review new registrations - New self-registered users appear in your resident management area for approval
Tip

Self-registration speeds up onboarding but ensure you verify new registrations against your actual resident list.

Step 3: Explore the Dashboard

Your admin dashboard gives you:

  • Today's overview - Visitor count, active residents, pending approvals
  • Manage Residents - Add, edit, or remove resident accounts
  • Manage Security - Control guard accounts and shifts
  • Notices - Post announcements to all residents
  • Reports - View visitor analytics and activity logs

Common Issues

I didn't receive an OTP

  • Check that you entered the correct phone number
  • Make sure your phone has network signal
  • Wait 60 seconds and tap "Resend OTP"
  • Contact your society admin if the problem persists

I can't find my society

  • Your society admin needs to add you to the system first
  • Double-check that you're using the registered phone number
  • Contact your society admin to verify your registration

The app is not loading

  • Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data)
  • Force close and reopen the app
  • Update to the latest version from the app store
