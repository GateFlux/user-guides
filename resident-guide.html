Resident Guide

Learn how to use GateFlux to manage visitors, track deliveries, and stay connected with your society.

Getting the App

Download the GateFlux Resident app from your device's app store:

iOS (iPhone/iPad)

Search for "GateFlux Resident" in the App Store and tap Get.

Android

Search for "GateFlux Resident" in Google Play and tap Install.

Logging In for the First Time

  1. Open the app and tap Get Started
  2. Enter your phone number – Use the number registered with your society
  3. Enter the OTP – You'll receive a 6-digit code via SMS
  4. Select your flat – If you have multiple units, choose the one you want to manage
  5. You're in! – You'll see your dashboard with visitor activity
Can't log in?

Make sure your phone number is registered with your society office. Contact your society admin if you need help.

Pre-Approving a Visitor

When you're expecting a guest, delivery, or service provider, pre-approve them so they can enter without delays:

  1. Tap the "+" button at the bottom of your screen
  2. Select visitor type:
    • Guest – Friends, family, personal visitors
    • Delivery – Amazon, Flipkart, Swiggy, Zomato, etc.
    • Service – Plumber, electrician, AC repair, etc.
    • Cab – Ola, Uber, or other ride services
  3. For Delivery/Service: Select the provider from the list (e.g., "Amazon", "Urban Company") or tap Other and type the name
  4. Enter visitor details:
    • Name (required)
    • Phone number (recommended)

Share with your visitor

After pre-approving, you can tap Share to send the entry code to your visitor via WhatsApp or SMS. They can show this code at the gate for quick entry.

When a Visitor Arrives

You'll get notifications when visitors check in and out:

Pre-Approved Visitors

If you already pre-approved the visitor:

  • The security guard will verify the code and allow entry
  • You'll receive a "Visitor Checked In" notification
  • You'll receive another notification when they check out

Unexpected Visitors (Walk-ins)

If someone arrives without pre-approval:

  1. You'll receive an approval request notification
  2. Tap the notification to see visitor details
  3. Choose Allow or Deny
  4. The guard will be notified of your decision

Viewing Visitor History

To see who has visited your flat:

  1. Tap Visitors in the bottom menu
  2. You'll see three tabs:
    • Expected – Pre-approved visitors who haven't arrived yet
    • Today – Today's check-ins and check-outs
    • History – All past visitors
  3. Tap any visitor to see details including entry/exit times and duration

Managing Recurring Visitors

For regular visitors like maids, cooks, or drivers, set up recurring access:

  1. Go to Visitors → Recurring tab
  2. Tap Add Recurring Visitor
  3. Enter their details and select the days/times they should have access
  4. Tap Save

Recurring visitors can check in on their scheduled days without needing approval each time.

Other Features

Notices & Announcements

Stay updated with society announcements:

  • Tap the bell icon to see all notices
  • Important notices appear as push notifications
  • You can filter by category (Maintenance, Events, Security, etc.)

Raising Complaints

Report issues to your society management:

  1. Go to More → Complaints
  2. Tap New Complaint
  3. Select category, describe the issue, and attach photos if needed
  4. Submit and track progress in the Complaints section

Viewing Your Dues

Check your maintenance payments:

  1. Go to More → Payments
  2. See outstanding dues and payment history
  3. Tap Pay Now to make online payments

App Settings

Customize your experience:

  • Notifications – Choose which alerts you want to receive
  • Family Members – Add family members who can also approve visitors
  • Profile – Update your contact information
  • Switch Flat – If you have multiple units, switch between them

Need Help?

If you're having trouble:

  • In-app help: Go to More → Help & Support
  • Contact society: Tap More → Contact Admin
  • FAQ: Check our Frequently Asked Questions
